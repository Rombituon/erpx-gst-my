Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for ERPX Malaysia GST and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app erpx_my_gst https://github.com/gentiger/erpx-gst-my
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app erpx_my_gst

Login to your site to configure the app.
